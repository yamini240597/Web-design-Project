Founders

Two friends from college started this journey together, for the love and passion that filled their hearts towards Succulents and Cacti.

Gretel Brights

My goal of building BeLeaf came from two things that brought me joy, gardening and art. I owe my passion for watching things grow to my grandmother, Wilma, who showed me how to be part of the miracle of nature.

Caitylyn Bennett

I always shared my mother's unique passion for cactus and succulent plants. From an early age, I curiously roamed the nursery fascinated by the unique shapes and colors of cactus and succulents. These unique shapes and colors are not the only thing I love, but also how they positively impact our lives.
